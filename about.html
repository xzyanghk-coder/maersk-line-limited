About Maersk Line, Limited

Team Maersk is a student group formed for a class project about container shipping, maritime transport, and global logistics. Our work is inspired by the operations and organization of Maersk.

Group Members & Roles

  • Benjamin Sawitsky – Project Manager
    Coordinates meetings, sets deadlines, and keeps the overall project on track.
  • Thomas Slaski – Research Lead
    Focuses on research about Maersk, container shipping routes, and case studies.
  • Kayleb Wray – Data & Content Specialist
    Collects data, organizes information, and helps draft written content for the project.
  • David Yang – Web & Presentation Designer
    Helps design the website layout and prepares the slides and visuals for the final presentation.

We work together as one team, but each member has a clear primary responsibility.

Our Project Goals

  1. Understand how Maersk organizes its global shipping network.
  2. Explore the basic structure of a shipping group or organization.
  3. Practice teamwork, time management, and clear division of tasks.

How We Work Together

We hold regular group meetings, use shared documents to track progress, and communicate through email and messaging apps. Each member is responsible for specific tasks, but we also support each other when someone needs help.
